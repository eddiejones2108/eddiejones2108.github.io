Intensional Datatype Refinements ‐ POPL21

In this paper, we present a higher-order program analysis that detects any pattern-matching errors. More importantly, however, unlike previous approaches, we tackle the issue of performance. By uncovering the right restrictions, we create a compositional analysis that is ultimately linear in the size of the program!

Its performance is witnessed not just by a formal complexity guarantee but also an efficient implementation as a GHC plugin.

The Dynamics of Dialects

My undergraduate dissertation focused on a different type of language - natural languages. And in particular, the remarkable way in which huge populations have agreed upon coherent systems for communication. It received a first-class mark of 87%.

I used a model of language acquisition to investigate how social networks influence the propagation of cultural symbols through a series of simulations. The most interesting result was that agents' propensity to distance themselves in response to a misunderstanding was a more a significant factor than the desire to connect with similar agents in the formation of homogenous communities. In the conclusion, I discussed the possible light this sheds on echo chambers.
